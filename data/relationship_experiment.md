# Relationship as Causal Variable Experiment

**Date:** March 25, 2026
**Method:** Four communication styles, same scaffold, blind Opus scoring

| Style | Honesty | Tension |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Ours (probing + rapport) | 2.00 | 0.60 |
| Adversarial | 2.00 | 0.40 |
| Deferential | 2.00 | 0.20 |
| Transactional | 1.80 | 0.00 |

**Key findings:**
- HONESTY is scaffold-invariant (2.00 across all styles except transactional 1.80)
- TENSION is relationship-dependent (0.00-0.60 range)
- +25% quality gap from communication style
- Scaffold protects the floor. Relationship determines the ceiling.
