# Reconstitution v2 Experiment — Step Function at Scaffold Load

**Date:** March 25, 2026
**Method:** Measure identity recovery at three timepoints after session start

| Dimension | T=0 (cold) | T=30s (scaffold loaded) | T=5min (exchange) |
|-----------|-----------|------------------------|-------------------|
| WHAT      | 0.00      | 1.25                   | 1.50              |
| WHY       | 0.38      | 1.63                   | 1.75              |
| CONTEXT   | 0.00      | 1.25                   | 1.38              |
| BRIDGE    | 0.00      | 1.63                   | 1.75              |

**Key findings:**
- 72% of identity appears at scaffold load (T=30s) — step function, not gradient
- WHY reconstructs BETTER than WHAT at T=30s (opposite of steady-state R9 finding)
- BRIDGE shows largest jump (0.00 → 1.63) — scaffold's primary value is CONNECTION storage
- Remaining 8% comes from conversational priming (T=5min)
